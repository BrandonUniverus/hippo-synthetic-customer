# Tenant & Lease Roster (Sub-metering & Rebilling)

Provided by: Northlake Property Management & Housing Operations. Synthetic/
illustrative. This describes the spaces where we **sub-meter and rebill** utility
costs to tenants — at the new Northlake Town Center (commercial) and at Cedar Row
Apartments (residential). For each space we note **how the cost is split**, in our
own terms; please map these to the appropriate allocation method.

## Northlake Town Center (mixed-use, commercial)

A newer mixed-use property: an enclosed retail hall, an office tower, a campus
annex, and shared infrastructure. We meter a **master** for each building and
allocate/sub-meter down to tenants, then roll everything up to a property total.

### Market Hall (retail) — master meter: Mall-Main (electric)

| Tenant / space | How we split the cost |
| --- | --- |
| Anchor Store | **Fixed** $2,000/month + share of common |
| Food Court | **15%** of the master bill |
| In-line Shop 1–4 | **By square footage** of each shop |
| Common Area | the remaining balance |

We add a small **overhead** to tenant charges (sometimes folded into the line,
sometimes shown separately).

### Office Tower — master meter: Tower-Main (electric)

| Tenant / space | How we split the cost |
| --- | --- |
| Floor 1–4 | **By each floor's sub-meter** (fully sub-metered) |
| Landlord / house | the **remainder** not covered by floor sub-meters |

### Campus Annex — master meter: Annex-Main (demand-metered)

| Tenant / space | How we split the cost |
| --- | --- |
| Bldg A / B / C | **By usage and coincident peak demand** |

### Substation feed — master: Substation-Master

| Tenant / space | How we split the cost |
| --- | --- |
| Substation 1 / 2 | **By percentage of metered usage** |

### Property Total (cost center)

All tenant charges **roll up** into a single Property Total cost center for
ownership reporting.

## Cedar Row Apartments (residential)

Two residential buildings with a shared common area and a solar carport. We want
to move from common-area-only billing to **unit-level sub-metering**.

| Building | Units | How we split |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cedar Row A | 60 | Electric **by unit sub-meter**; water **by unit / occupancy** (winter-capped); common-area lighting as overhead |
| Cedar Row B | 64 | Same approach |

## How we generate tenant bills

We produce tenant bills monthly from the allocated amounts, and we **reconcile**
the sum of tenant charges back to the master/parent utility bill so nothing is
over- or under-recovered. Some spaces we'd want generated automatically each
period; others on demand.
