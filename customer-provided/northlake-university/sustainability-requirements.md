# Sustainability Reporting Requirements

Provided by: Northlake University Sustainability Office. Synthetic/illustrative.
This describes the sustainability reporting we need the system to support:
ENERGY STAR benchmarking, greenhouse-gas (GHG) emissions, and our targets.

## ENERGY STAR Portfolio Manager

We benchmark our properties in ENERGY STAR Portfolio Manager and want the system
to support property setup, rating requests, and score history.

| Property | Primary function | Score expectation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Northlake Main Campus (parent) | College/University | 1–100 score |
| Admin Hall | Office | 1–100 score |
| Science Center | Laboratory | metrics only (no 1–100 score) |
| Library | Library | 1–100 score |
| Student Center | Food Service | 1–100 score |
| Cedar Row Apartments | Multifamily Housing (Residence Hall) | 1–100 score |

We understand **scores come from Portfolio Manager** (not entered by hand). Please
plan to connect our Portfolio Manager account (a test/sandbox account is fine for
setup) and synchronize scores; we want **quarterly** score history for 2024–2025
showing a modest improvement trend.

## Greenhouse-gas emissions

We report emissions by scope and want them computed from our usage:

| Scope | Sources | Factor basis |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Scope 1 (direct) | Natural gas; thermal plant combustion | EPA stationary combustion |
| Scope 2 (purchased) | Purchased electricity | eGRID (Sacramento/CAMX region) |
| Scope 3 (indirect) | Water/sewer embedded energy | conveyance + treatment factor |

- We want emission factors maintained with **effective dates** (a factor can
  change between 2024 and 2025).
- Onsite **solar** should show as a **market-based Scope 2 reduction**; we
  **retain the RECs** (per the Helios PPA), so please reflect that in market-based
  accounting and avoided-emissions reporting.

## Targets & cadence

- Track building **energy use intensity (EUI)** and **cost intensity**, ranked
  across our buildings.
- Annual emissions report by scope and property; quarterly ENERGY STAR scores.
- We have energy projects (LED retrofit, chiller upgrade, solar expansion,
  recommissioning) and want **measurement & verification** against a baseline.
