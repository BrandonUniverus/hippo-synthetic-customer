# Rate Tariff Sheet — River City Utilities (Water / Sewer / Stormwater)

Provided by: Northlake University Finance. Synthetic/illustrative values for
setup. Provider: River City Utilities (RCU), municipal multi-service. One account
and one monthly statement carry **three services**. Only water is metered; sewer
volume is derived from metered water; stormwater is a flat area-based fee.

**Effective Jan 1, 2024 – Dec 31, 2025**

## Water — Non-Residential, tiered (RCU-W-TIER)

| Charge | Basis | Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Meter charge — 1 in | per month | $42.00 |
| Meter charge — 2 in | per month | $96.00 |
| Meter charge — 4 in | per month | $260.00 |
| Usage Tier 1 | per kgal, 0–50 | $4.85 |
| Usage Tier 2 | per kgal, 50–200 | $6.40 |
| Usage Tier 3 | per kgal, over 200 | $8.15 |

## Sewer — Volumetric on water (RCU-S-VOL)

| Charge | Basis | Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Base charge | per month | $28.50 |
| Volume | per kgal of metered water | $7.95 |

Apartments are capped at a winter-average volume for the sewer charge.

## Stormwater — Impervious area (RCU-SW-ERU)

| Charge | Basis | Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ERU charge | per ERU per month | $11.20 |

ERU = equivalent runoff unit (impervious area). Applies where stormwater service
exists; **Cedar Row buildings may not have a stormwater charge — to confirm.**
