# Rate Tariff Sheet — Valley Electric District (Electric)

Provided by: Northlake University Finance. Synthetic/illustrative values for
setup. Provider: Valley Electric District (VED), municipal electric distribution.

## Schedule: Time-of-Use General Service (VED-TOU-GS)

Applies to all Northlake electric accounts on the VED service. Demand is billed on
the highest 15-minute average kW in the period. Power factor below 0.95 on
lab-heavy buildings can add a reactive-power adjustment.
